AI Ping Pong game



Instructions

1 : Keep your right wrist visible in camera .
2 : Move your right wrist up and down to move the paddle .
3 : With each hit of the ball on your paddle , the ball's speed will increase .
4 : Get four points to win by hitting the screen behind the computer's paddle .
5 : If the computer gets four points , you lose !
6 : Go on and play !
